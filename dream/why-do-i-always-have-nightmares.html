Frequently asked

Why do I keep having nightmares?

Nightmares are almost always linked to unprocessed emotional material — stress, anxiety, trauma, or grief that hasn't found adequate expression while awake. They're the mind working overtime on what the waking self hasn't fully dealt with.

Do nightmares mean something bad will happen?

No — nightmares are retrospective, not prophetic. They process past experiences and current emotional states; they don't predict future events.

How do I reduce nightmares?

Addressing the underlying material is more effective than trying to change the dreams directly. Stress reduction, processing difficult emotions while awake, and consistent sleep hygiene all tend to reduce nightmare frequency over time.
