Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Thank you for using Zeus Tarot. We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. Please read this policy carefully before subscribing.

Quick summary: We offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase if you have not used your subscription. Trial purchases ($1.00) are non-refundable once the readings have been used.

1. Subscription Refunds (Monthly and Yearly)

You may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase if:

  • You have not used any deep AI readings during that period, or
  • The service was unavailable for an extended period due to technical issues on our end

After 7 days, or after using 3 or more deep readings, subscriptions are non-refundable. You may cancel at any time to prevent future charges, and access will continue until the end of the paid period.

2. Trial Refunds ($1.00)

Trial purchases are non-refundable once any reading has been accessed. If you experienced a technical error that prevented you from receiving your readings, please contact us and we will review your case.

3. Duplicate Charges

If you were charged more than once for the same subscription period due to a technical error, you are entitled to a full refund of the duplicate charge. Please contact us with your purchase details.

4. Service Unavailability

If Zeus Tarot is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours due to issues on our end, affected subscribers may request a prorated credit or refund for the downtime period.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at omegafund01@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your email address used for purchase
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Order number (available in your Lemon Squeezy receipt email)

We will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days and will be returned to your original payment method.

6. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card provider. Most issues can be resolved quickly through direct communication. Chargebacks that are initiated without prior contact may result in suspension of your account.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting to this page. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy: omegafund01@gmail.com

We aim to respond within 3 business days.
