Frequently asked

Is it common to feel disconnected during intimacy?

Yes, this is a common experience and can result from a wide range of causes, including stress, body image concerns, unresolved relational tension, or past experiences that affect present-moment presence.

Does this disconnection mean something is wrong with the relationship?

Not necessarily — it can be about internal factors unrelated to the relationship's health. That said, if it persists, an open conversation with your partner can help clarify what's happening.

What can help with feeling more present during intimacy?

Addressing underlying stress, discussing the experience openly with a partner, and in some cases speaking with a therapist who specializes in intimacy, can all support a return to fuller presence.
